SAE Attribution model

Manages the assignment of students to SAE (Situation d'Apprentissage et d'Ã‰valuation) by supervisors (responsables). Handles creation, retrieval, and deletion of assignments, as well as validation to ensure a SAE can only be assigned by one supervisor and students cannot be assigned to the same SAE multiple times.
